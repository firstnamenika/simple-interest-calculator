To determine the simple accrued interest, multiply the account balance by the interest rate and the duration the funds remain in the account. The formula for calculating simple interest is as follows:

    Interest = P * R * T.
    Where:

  • P stands for the principal amount, i.e., the initial balance.
  • R represents the interest rate, usually expressed as a decimal per year.
  • T signifies the number of time periods, typically measured in one-year intervals.
